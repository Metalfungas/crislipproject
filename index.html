The Hypnotists

Gordon Korman • Cinematic Project

Would You Be Able to Handle the Power?

In a world where the mind can be controlled, nothing is truly safe. The Hypnotists follows a group of kids who discover abilities far beyond what should be possible — but power always comes with consequences. As experiments unfold and control slips away, trust becomes dangerous and reality starts to blur.
